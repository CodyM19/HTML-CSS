Have you ever made a pizza from scratch ?

Yeah, but have you really thought about it ?

Like, how if you put the sauce on first, before you make the base it doesn't work?

Or, if you put the cheese on then the sauce it's messed up too.

Making a pizza is all about the order of operations. You need to get the dough right first, then add the sauce, followed by the cheese and toppings. It's a delicate balance that requires attention to detail and a bit of culinary skill.

Pizzas are sort of like lines of code. The process of making a pizza is almost always created in the same order of intsructions.

This is the common order of operations.

We will list them here.

  1. Make the pizza base.
  2. Add the sauce.
  3. Add the cheese.
  4. Add the toppings.
  5. Bake the pizza.
  6. Slice and serve.
